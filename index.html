ABOUT US

The Forum of Data Science Engineers (FODSE), a premium club at SVCE, established on December 6, 2020, through collaboration between IETE and AIT, SVCE. Dedicated to students passionate about Data Science, Data Analytics, ML, AI, and Statistics, FODSE encourages skill development and project creation with guidance from forum mentors. The club organizes webinars, guest lectures, and technical events like hackathons, workshops, quizzes, and case studies to foster a goal of transforming data into insights.
